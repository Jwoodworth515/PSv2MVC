Musician Things

Here are a few of my projects from my day's as a musician.

Click on one of the links to view a collection of music and pictures

Defiance Pointe

As one of the founding members of this project, I can say that we went against the grain and everyone's warnings and chased our dreams with this one. At one point we were signed to an indie label and touring off of our debut record Until the Dawn. I wrote all of the music and did most of the artwork design and creation.

Victory for Villains

After Defiance Pointe went on a hiatus and had some member changes we rebranded as Victory for Villains and picked up right where we left off. The ep here was never released on any platform.

From Skies of Fire

After the disband of Victory for Villains (Defiance Pointe 2.0) I filled the guitar role for From Skies of Fire. I designed the album artwork for this album "No Coast E.P.

Give Them my Name

Ever live in a house with ten other people and 60% of them are musicians? Well you eventually start a band in the garage and make make enough noise to have the boy in blue constantly asking your for your name and to keep it down. Thus, Give Them my Name was created.
